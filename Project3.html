library(tidyverse)
library(ggplot2)

DataSet Used

I used the MyAnimeList Top 2000 Anime dataset found on Kaggle.

This dataset compiles the top 2000 anime from the website [MyAnimeList] (https://myanimelist.net/).

Variables in the Dataset

11 variables of information are provided:

  • ‘Name’: the name of the Anime
  • ‘Type’: the entry’s type (TV, Movie, or Music)
  • ‘Score’: the score of the anime based on 4 characteristics produced by MyAnimeList
  • ‘Rank’: the rank of the anime based on it’s score
  • ‘Popularity Rank’: the rank of the anime based on it’s popularity

Variables in the Dataset

  • ‘Air Date’: the time frame of which the anime aired
  • ‘Studio’: the studio that worked on the anime
  • ‘Number of Episodes’: the number of episodes in each anime
  • ‘Genre’: the genre of the anime
  • ‘Themes’: the theme of the anime
  • ‘Demographics’: the anime’s target audience
